A Treeplanting Rehab

Treehab is a therapeutic work placement for offenders on conditional release. The project will partner with ‘Aghel Nebun in Prince George and offer three graduates of the CRF jobs as tree planters during the 2021 spring planting season. Each participant will be paired with a veteran tree planter with at least five years of experience. Planters will be paid per tree at the industry rate, incentivizing hard work ethic.

Treehab operates under the Tree Amigos Workers Co-op
